<p>
BikeSharingHub displays the availability of shared bikes in your city (the list follows).
</p>
<p>
It is a fork of the <a href="https://f-droid.org/fr/packages/be.brunoparmentier.openbikesharing.app/">OpenBikeSharing</a> application, with few improvements for recent Android versions.
</p>
<p>
